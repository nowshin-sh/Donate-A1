Our Donate Plan

We are mobilizing resources to assist the severely affected population in chosen Upazilas of Sylhet and Sunamgonj districts.
Our focus is on delivering immediate aid in the form of emergency food and hygiene kits, comprising essential items such as
toilet and laundry soaps, sanitary napkins, oral rehydration salts, and water purifying tablets, to 1300 identified households.

Food

We aim to address the urgent needs of the affected population by providing essential relief supplies. This includes distributing food, clean water, clothing, and temporary shelter to ensure the basic well-being of those impacted. We prioritize efficient distribution by collaborating closely with local organizations and government agencies, ensuring that aid reaches those who need it most promptly.

Shelter

To address the displacement of families, we are allocating funds for the construction and repair of temporary shelters. This initiative aims to provide immediate housing solutions, ensuring the safety and comfort of those who have been displaced.

Education

Recognizing the vital role education plays in recovery, we are committed to supporting the reopening of schools in the affected areas. Our efforts extend to providing necessary educational materials for children who have faced disruptions due to the disaster.
